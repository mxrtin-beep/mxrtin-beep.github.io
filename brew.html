Firestone Walker Pivo Pils

5D. German Pilsner -- April 20, 2023

Grain

  • 10 kg Weyermann Pilsner (95%)
  • 0.53 kg Weyermann Carafoam (5%)

Hops

  • 52.5 g Magnum (60 minutes boil)
  • 35 g German Spalt Select (30 minutes boil)
  • 55 g Saphir (0 minutes boil)
  • 55 g Saphir (8 days dry hop)

Yeast

1 Packet SafLager West European Lager

Extras

  • 75 g Fermax Yeast Nutrient
  • 3 Whirfloc tablets

Mash

  • Mash In: 63C for 15 minutes
  • Mash Step: 68C for 45 minutes
  • Mash Out: 75C for 10 minutes

Fermentation

  • Fermentation 1: 10C for 14 days
  • D Rest: 18C for 4 days
  • Slow crash: 4C for 5 days
  • Transfer and lager: 4C for 14 days
  • Dry hop: 9C for 7 days
Initial SG: 1.041
Final SG: 1.012
ABV: 3.81%

Notes

  • Delicious. Smooth summer beer.
